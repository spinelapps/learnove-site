Your privacy is our priority. Learnova is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data whatsoever.
1

Data We Do Not Collect

Learnova Grammar App is built with a privacy-first approach. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information through Google Play or any other third-party platforms.
  • Store or process analytics data, behavioral data, or user activity logs.
  • Request access to your contacts, location, camera, or any device sensors.

Your usage of the app is entirely private and remains on your device at all times.

2

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since no data is collected in the first place, there is nothing to share.

No advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics companies receive any information from Learnova.

3

Security Measures

While we do not collect or store user data, we take the security of the app seriously and implement measures including:

  • Preventing unauthorized access to app functionalities.
  • Regular code reviews to ensure no unintended data exposure.
  • Ensuring all app content is served securely and locally.
4

Your Rights

Since no data is collected, you retain complete control over your experience within the app. All progress data, quiz results, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted externally.

You may uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored data from your device.

5

Third-Party Services

Learnova Grammar App does not integrate with any third-party services, trackers, or analytics tools. The app operates fully offline and does not make any network requests that could result in data collection.

Any links to external resources are for informational purposes only and do not involve data collection on our part.

6

Policy Updates

This User Data Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in app functionality or applicable legal requirements. Any significant changes will be communicated within the app.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this User Data Policy, we're happy to help.
